Our Mission

Digitizing The Divine City

Vihara is a community-led initiative to connect tourists with the authentic local businesses of Shravanabelagola.

Why we started Vihara

Every year, lakhs of devotees visit Shravanabelagola, but they often struggle to find authentic local shops, clean messes, and hidden heritage spots because these businesses aren't online.

Vihara bridges this gap. We provide a free digital platform for small shopkeepers to showcase their business, helping tourists discover the real essence of our town.
