Our Mission

Digitizing The Divine City

Vihara is a community-led initiative to connect tourists with the authentic local businesses of Shravanabelagola.

The Vision

It starts with Shravanabelagola, but the dream is bigger. Our mission is to put every small town, hidden village, and local artisan of India on the digital map.

The Gap

India's true essence lives in its towns. Yet, authentic shops and historic spots remain invisible to the internet. We are building the bridge to connect them.

Empowering Locals

Technology shouldn't just be for big cities. We give a digital identity to the small merchant, the mess owner, and the farmer, helping them thrive in a modern world.
