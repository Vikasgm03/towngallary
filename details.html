Cow Farm Sugganahalli

The Chandragiri Hill is one of the two hills at Shravanabelagola, Karnataka, famous for its collection of ancient Jain temples and inscriptions. It holds great historical and religious importance, showcasing the grandeur of Jain architecture and the devotion of ancient kings.

The hill offers a mesmerizing panoramic view of the town and is a must-visit spot for photographers, pilgrims, and tourists seeking spiritual peace.
